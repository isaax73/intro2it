About Me

A detailed personal biography that tells the story of who I am.

My name is Islam, and I am a passionate individual with a strong background in technology and a keen interest in innovation. Born in Nukus, Karakalpakistan, on December 5th, 2006, I moved to Tashkent in 2012 and have lived here since. Growing up in a tech-savvy environment, I developed a love for coding at a young age, which has guided my journey so far.

More About Me

I graduated from the 17th School in Tashkent and then enrolled at Inha University in the School of Computer and Information Engineering. Alongside my university studies, I attended IT-LABS tech school to enhance my programming skills, where I studied both frontend and backend development. Later, I taught myself Python, further fueling my enthusiasm for coding. My interest lies not only in coding but also in reading books related to IT and technology. I live in a family of five, being the oldest child, which has taught me responsibility and leadership. My hobbies include playing football and reading comics, which help me unwind. I would describe myself as a calm person, though I do occasionally get angry or nervous, like anyone else. My journey has been marked by continuous learning and a desire to explore new horizons in the tech industry, and I look forward to what's next.
